Pre-Processing Center

The purchased raw material is processed in a pre-processing center. The workers handle the materials on a table where it is peeled and separated according to its size or grades using a grading machine or manually. The unit has sufficient pre-processing area with good chill room facility.

Processing Center

The Company facilitates a large area for processing the pre-processed raw material. The processing center is integrated with the pre-processing center. A large area reduces the congestion and provides for easy material and labour movement. It also maintains the freshness and quality of the materials. Skilled labour force is the strength of the processing center.

Freezing Facilities

A well planned freezer area is provided inside the company which is integrated to Processing center. Company facility includes 3 modern Contact Freezers and a Blast Freezer with a total per day capacity of 27 MT.

Storage Facilities

Company provides a Cold Store integrated to its packing section. The cold store with a capacity of 150MT is sufficient for the total freezing needs of the company.
